About Us

Master Your Financial Destiny With Us

R S KUMAR & ASSOCIATES is a Chartered Accountant firm based in Chennai, established in 1986 by CA. S. Gopinath & CA. R. Sivakumar. Over the years, a team of dedicated professionals joined to form the coveted GKA Family.

We work closely with clients to understand their unique needs and provide customized solutions to help achieve their financial goals and objectives.

Our experienced Chartered Accountants deliver personalized solutions — ensuring your financial journey is smooth, compliant and optimized for growth.
